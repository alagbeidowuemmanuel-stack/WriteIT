Common Questions

Get quick answers to the questions we hear most often

What types of writing services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of writing services including academic writing (essays, research papers, dissertations), business writing (reports, proposals, business plans), content creation (blog posts, articles, web content), technical writing, creative writing, and proofreading/editing services.

How do I know my work will be original?

We guarantee 100% original content for all our writing services. Every piece is crafted from scratch according to your specific requirements. We use advanced plagiarism detection software to ensure all work is completely original before delivery. You'll receive a plagiarism report with your completed work.

Are your writers qualified?

Yes, all our writers are highly qualified professionals with advanced degrees in their respective fields. They undergo a rigorous selection process and continuous quality assessments. Our team includes PhD holders, industry experts, and professional writers with years of experience in academic and business writing.

Is my personal information safe with you?

Absolutely. We take client confidentiality very seriously. All your personal information and project details are protected by strict privacy policies and secure systems. We never share your information with third parties, and all communications are encrypted for your security.

Can you handle urgent deadlines?

Yes, we offer rush order services for urgent projects. Depending on the complexity and length of your project, we can deliver within 24 hours for most writing tasks. Rush orders may incur additional fees, which will be clearly communicated before you confirm your order.

Do you offer editing for existing documents?

Yes, we provide comprehensive editing and proofreading services for existing documents. Our editors will improve grammar, spelling, punctuation, style, clarity, and overall flow while maintaining your original voice and message. We also offer formatting services for various citation styles.

Can I request specific formatting styles?

Absolutely. We're proficient in all major formatting styles including APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, IEEE, and more. Simply specify your preferred style when placing your order, and we'll ensure your document is perfectly formatted according to those guidelines.

Do you provide research for academic papers?

Yes, comprehensive research is included in all our academic writing services. Our writers have access to academic databases and resources to conduct thorough research for your papers. We can also work with specific sources you provide if required.

How is pricing determined?

Our pricing is based on several factors including the type of service, academic level, number of pages/words, deadline urgency, and complexity of the topic. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You'll receive a detailed quote before any work begins.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for larger projects. All payments are processed through secure payment gateways to ensure your financial information is protected.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for large projects or ongoing work. If you have multiple assignments or need regular writing services, contact us for custom pricing. We also offer special rates for returning clients and long-term partnerships.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the initial draft, we provide free revisions until you're satisfied. In rare cases where we cannot meet your requirements, we offer partial or full refunds according to our refund policy.

What is your typical process?

Our process involves: 1) Initial consultation and project briefing, 2) Writer assignment based on expertise, 3) Research and outline creation, 4) Writing and drafting, 5) Quality checks and editing, 6) Delivery of first draft, 7) Revisions if needed, 8) Final delivery with all supporting documents.

How will I receive my completed work?

Completed work is delivered electronically to your registered email address in your preferred format (typically Word document or PDF). You'll also have access to your client dashboard where you can download your files, track order progress, and communicate with your writer.

Can I communicate with my writer?

Yes, we encourage communication between clients and writers. You can communicate directly with your assigned writer through our secure messaging system to provide additional instructions, ask questions, or request updates on your project's progress.

What if I need revisions?

We offer free revisions within 14 days of delivery. Simply request changes through your client dashboard, and your writer will make the necessary adjustments. There's no limit to the number of revision requests for premium packages, and standard packages include 2 free revisions.
